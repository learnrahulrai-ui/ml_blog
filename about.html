About This Blog

Who Am I

Hi, I'm Rahul Rai. I'm interested in machine learning — not the hype version, the actual nuts-and-bolts version. What's a gradient? Why does this layer shape work and that one doesn't? Why did the loss suddenly spike at epoch 47?

This blog is me thinking in public. I write posts when I figure something out or when I've spent too long confused about something and finally get it. If it helps someone else avoid three hours of confusion, great.

What This Blog Is

  • Notes on ML fundamentals — backprop, attention, optimization
  • Paper summaries with actual explanations (not just abstracts)
  • Code that I actually ran, producing results that actually work
  • Honest about what I don't know

What This Blog Isn't

  • AI hype coverage
  • Tutorial-farm content with 30 headers and no depth
  • Sponsored anything

The Site

Pure HTML and CSS. No JavaScript, no tracking, no cookies, no frameworks. The 90s called and their web architecture was actually fine for a blog. Fast, readable, works everywhere. Hosted on GitHub Pages.
