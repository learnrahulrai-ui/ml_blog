Who Am I

Hi, I'm Rahul Rai. I'm teaching myself machine learning — not the hype version, the actual nuts-and-bolts version. What is a model, really? Why hide part of the data? What is the machine actually doing when it "learns"?

This blog is me thinking in public. I made one rule: no jargon until I've earned it. If I can't explain something in words a farmer in 1953 would understand, I don't actually understand it yet. So I ban the buzzwords, draw the idea, solve it by pencil, then write the code — and only at the very end do I stick the textbook labels on.

What This Blog Is

  • ML fundamentals, derived by hand before any library is imported
  • ASCII diagrams and pencil math, then the real Python
  • Code that I actually ran, producing results that actually work
  • Honest about what I don't know

The Series So Far

  • Lab 1 — Guess a House Price: build two rules from scratch (ask-closest and straight-stick) on the California housing pile, by hand.
  • Lab 2 — coming soon: sort into yes or no — the S-curve and where to draw the line.

What This Blog Isn't

  • AI hype coverage
  • Tutorial-farm content with 30 headers and no depth
  • Sponsored anything
